Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need programming skills to use Chatflux.io?

No, Chatflux.io is designed to be user-friendly and does not require any coding skills.

What types of documents can I use to create a chatbot?

You can use any document with text, such as a PDF, text should be OCRable.

How does the chatbot learn from my document?

Chatflux.io uses advanced natural language processing (NLP) algorithms to analyze the text in your document and identify key information.

Is it possible to share the chatbot I created with others?

Absolutely! By default, any chatbot you create is set to private, but you can easily change the settings to make it public and share it with anyone you want.

Can I integrate the chatbot with my website or other platforms?

Yes, Chatflux.io provides easy websites integration, allowing you to reach your audience where they are.

Can Chatflux support multiple languages?

Yes! Chatflux is designed to accommodate a variety of languages, including English, French, Spanish, Arabic, Italian, Dutch, and many more. The bot will try to answer in the language of your question, even if the document is in a different language.

Where is my data stored?

Your document's content is hosted on secure AWS servers, but Chatflux.io does not store the document itself anywhere, ensuring the highest level of security and privacy for your data.

Is it possible to customize my chatbot's responses and behavior?

Absolutely! With Chatflux.io, you can easily edit the base prompt and personalize your chatbot's name, tone, and even language preferences. For example, you can instruct your chatbot to respond only in a specific language or to provide more detailed information on a particular topic. The possibilities are endless!
