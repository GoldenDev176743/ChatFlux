Pricing FAQ

What do you mean by importing data from YouTube videos and websites?

Chatflux offers the ability to import data directly from YouTube videos and websites to train your chatbots. This means you can utilize video transcripts or web page content to train your chatbots.

Can I integrate my chatbots in my websites?

Yes, to integrate a chatbot using Chatflux, all you need is a one-line script tag provided by Chatflux in the chatbot settings. Simply add this script tag to your website's html, and your custom chatbot will be seamlessly integrated, allowing your visitors to interact with it without any complex technical setup on your part.

What is the significance of making chatbots private or public in Chatflux?

Chatflux lets you decide whether your chatbots should be private or public. Private chatbots are accessible only to you, ensuring confidential information stays secure. Public chatbots are accessible to anyone who visits your chatbot's link, enabling you to provide information and assistance to a wider audience.

Can I customize the appearance of my embedded chatbot with Chatflux?

Yes, The Sky is the limit plan allows you to customize the appearance of your embedded chatbot to match your website's design and branding. This ensures a cohesive look and feel, enhancing the overall user experience.

How does the no Chatflux branding feature benefit my business?

The no Chatflux branding feature allows you to maintain a cohesive and professional appearance for your embedded chatbot. By removing any Chatflux branding, your chatbot becomes an integral part of your website, aligning with your brand identity and creating a seamless user experience for your visitors.

Can I monitor my chatbot's interactions and usage in Chatflux?

Yes, Chatflux provides insights and analytics on your chatbot's performance and usage. These metrics help you understand how your chatbot is performing to better serve your users and achieve your business goals.
